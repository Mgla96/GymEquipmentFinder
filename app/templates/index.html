What This Tracks

This site tracks deals from Rogue, Fringe Sports, REP, Vulcan, Titan Fitness, and XMark. When an item goes in stock on these websites, it will automatically be updated here. Webpage is updated 3 times daily(8:30-9 am, 3-3:30 pm, and 9-9:30pm Pacific Time) as to not burden these companies with too many requests to their website. If a section is blank that means everything is sold out. I hope you find this page useful!
